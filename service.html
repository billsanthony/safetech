Surveillance and counter-surveillance services
Detection programs
Personal security details
Emergency rapid response service/patrol team

Where is your corporate headquarters located?
Our corporate headquarters is located at Safetech Plaza, No. 3 Chike Nwosu Street, Near Love FM, Mpape Hills, Abuja, Nigeria.
What does your dignitary and executive protection service include?
Our dignitary and executive protection services include:
Personal security details
Close protection units for high and low threat environments
Intra and international travel protection
Advance team activities
"Close-in-Team" operations
Motorcade escort services
How do you vet your security personnel?
We conduct stringent pre-employment checks, including:
Address verification and character reference checks from neighbors
Background screening extending to education and local government verification
Continuous vetting to keep our employee database updated
Vetting services for our clients’ recruitment needs
What services do you provide for special events or crowd control?
We offer specialized guards for crowd control and individual safety protection, ensuring the safety of guests during special events, especially those that might attract undesirable personalities.
How do you respond to security breaches?
Our emergency response services can include the use of horses and dogs when deemed appropriate. We prioritize rapid response to any security breach.
What is your emergency rapid response service?
Our emergency rapid response service operates 24/7, ensuring that we can swiftly react to any emergency or distress call from clients. We aim to reach the site promptly with the appropriate rescue team.
What kind of training do you provide for your staff?
We offer comprehensive training programs that focus on:
Adherence to assignment schedules Grooming, behavior, and discipline
Effective teamwork and needs-based training
We also provide fire fighting and prevention training, covering safety hazards, fire types, and proper use of fire extinguishers.
What is your approach to surveillance systems?
We use surveillance systems (CCTV) to identify and monitor unauthorized movements, preempt incidents, and record activities within premises. Stored images can be referenced later for investigations.
What fire alarm systems do you provide?
Our conventional fire alarm systems consist of smoke (photoelectric) and heat detectors located at designated points to detect smoke or heat early, preventing fire outbreaks.
